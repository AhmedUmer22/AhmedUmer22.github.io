FAQ

Common questions.

Does Sophie replace my receptionist?

No — Sophie handles overflow, after-hours, and peak periods. Your human staff focus on patients in the clinic. Think of Sophie as a second receptionist who never calls in sick.

How does call forwarding work?

You forward your existing clinic number to Sophie's number. It takes 2 minutes and can be toggled on or off anytime. No new hardware needed.

What if Sophie can't answer something?

Sophie is trained to say "I'll make a note and have our team follow up with you." Nothing slips through the cracks — every interaction is logged.

How long does setup take?

We go live within 48 hours of your onboarding call. All you need is a Google account and 30 minutes of your time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Pause or cancel with 30 days notice at any time.

What practice management software do you support?

We integrate with Google Calendar out of the box. Dentrix, Eaglesoft, and Open Dental integrations are available on the Done For You plan.
